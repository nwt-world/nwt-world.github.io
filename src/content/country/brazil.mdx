## 📝 Notes

### 🌍 Why Visit Brazil?
Brazil has enormous travel potential because it is almost a continent by itself.
But if you consider calm itineraries, a well planned routes to specific regions should be considered. 

---

### ⚠️ Safety
Brazil is not a low-attention destination.
Brazil can be rewarding, but safety must shape the route.

---

### 💡 Who This Country Is For
Travelers who want large-scale nature, wildlife, and culture — and are willing to plan around safety rather than ignore it.
