## 📝 Notes

### 🌍 Why Visit Peru?
Peru is one of the clearest “must-consider” countries in South America if you value ancient history, mountains, and high-altitude landscapes.

Its main strength is not only Machu Picchu itself, but the whole Andean route around it: Cusco, the Sacred Valley, ruins, villages, viewpoints, and mountain excursions.

---

### 🏔️ Altitude and Pacing
Peru should not be rushed.

Cusco, Rainbow Mountain, Humantay Lake, Lake Titicaca, and many Andean viewpoints involve serious altitude.  
You need time to acclimatize before doing high-altitude hikes or long excursions.

---

### ⚠️ Safety
Peru is manageable on the main tourist routes, but safety and comfort vary by region.

Cusco, Sacred Valley, Arequipa, and classic tourist corridors are generally workable with normal awareness.  
Lima, transport hubs, and less established regions require more caution.

---

### 💡 Who This Country Is For
Travelers who want a focused South America trip built around Inca history, Machu Picchu, and high-Andes landscapes.
