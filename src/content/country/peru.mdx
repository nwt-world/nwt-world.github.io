## 📝 Notes

### 🌍 Why Visit Peru?
Peru is one of the clearest “must-consider” countries in South America if you value ancient history and mountain scenery.

Its main strength is the combination of:
- Cusco and Inca heritage  
- Machu Picchu  
- Sacred Valley towns and ruins  
- high-Andes day trips and viewpoints  

The country has many other regions, but the Cusco route is the natural core.

---

### 🏔️ Altitude and Pacing
Peru should not be rushed.

Cusco, the Sacred Valley, Rainbow Mountain, Humantay Lake, and Lake Titicaca all involve significant altitude.  
The itinerary needs acclimatization time, especially before hikes above 4,000–5,000 meters.

This is one of the main reasons to plan Peru carefully.
