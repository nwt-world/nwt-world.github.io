## 📝 Notes

### ☀️ Predictable Beach Escape
Aruba is about reliable weather and easy relaxation.  
It sits outside the main hurricane belt, which makes sunshine more predictable than on many Caribbean islands.
Infrastructure is smooth, distances are short, and daily life feels simple.
