## 📝 Notes

### ✈️ A Major Regional Hub
Bogotá is one of the most important air hubs in Latin America.  
Many routes across the Americas pass through Colombia, so the country can naturally appear in a longer South America itinerary.

---

### ⚠️ Safety
Colombia is not a low-attention destination.
Safety varies sharply by region, city, neighborhood, and time of day.
