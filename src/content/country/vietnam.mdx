Vietnam is not a single, uniform destination.

- **Northern Vietnam:** cooler winters, hot and humid summers; most comfortable in spring and autumn
- **Central Vietnam:** distinct rainy season with occasional strong storms; best in summer months
- **Southern Vietnam:** tropical climate with wet and dry seasons; most pleasant in winter

This regional diversity is a major advantage for long stays: when one part of the country is less comfortable, another is often at its best.
