## 📝 Notes

### 🌍 Why Visit Chile?
Chile is one of the strongest nature countries in South America if you value mountains, volcanoes, desert, glaciers, and clean logistics.
It feels more stable and structured than many regional destinations, which makes it easier to plan.

---

### 💰 Costs and Practical Reality
Chile has never been a budget destination.
Prices are higher than in many South American countries, but they are generally more stable and predictable.  

---

### 💡 Who This Country Is For
Travelers who want strong Andes and volcano nature in a stable, well-organized South American country.
