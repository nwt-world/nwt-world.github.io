## 📝 Notes

### 🌍 Why Visit Bolivia?
Bolivia is one of South America’s most visually extreme countries.
It feels rough, simple, and far less polished than nearby countries.

---

### ⚠️ Reliability and Civil Unrest
Bolivia is not just “challenging” — it can become logistically unreliable.
Roadblocks, protests, strikes, fuel shortages, and sudden route closures can disrupt travel with little warning.
Do not build a tight itinerary around it without checking the current situation first.

---

### 🏔️ Altitude and Comfort
Most of the strongest places in Bolivia are high, cold, and remote.

Be ready for:
- altitude above 3,500–4,500 meters  
- basic accommodation on remote routes  
- long drives  
- limited heating and comfort  
- weather that can feel harsh even outside winter  

---

### 💡 Who This Country Is For
Travelers who want extreme high-altitude landscapes and are willing to accept rough logistics, altitude, and possible disruptions.
