## 📝 Notes

### 🐢 Galápagos
The Galápagos Islands are Ecuador’s strongest and most unique reason to visit.
They are expensive and logistically separate from the mainland, but they offer wildlife experiences that are difficult to replace elsewhere.
For many travelers, Galápagos alone can justify the whole trip.

---

### ⚠️ Safety
Ecuador needs careful route planning.
Galápagos and many inland highland routes feel more manageable, while parts of the coast, border regions, and some urban areas require more caution.
