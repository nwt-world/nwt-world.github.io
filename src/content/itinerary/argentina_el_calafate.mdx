## Overview

A classic Patagonian itinerary combining **El Calafate**,
the spectacular **Perito Moreno Glacier**, and
the mountain village of **El Chaltén**.

This route focuses on **glaciers, mountains, and open Patagonian landscapes**.
It is compact, visually impressive, and works well even with limited time.

**Province:** Santa Cruz  
**Overall time:** 3–5 days  
**Main focus:** glaciers, mountain scenery, hiking (optional)  
**Ranking:** top Patagonian highlight  
**Best season:** October–March

## Transportation

- **Best option:** rented car (maximum flexibility)
- Organized tours work well for Perito Moreno Glacier
- Buses operate between El Calafate and El Chaltén
- No public transport from the airport — taxi or transfer required

---

## Connections

- El Calafate → Ushuaia (frequent direct flights)
- El Calafate → Bariloche (direct flights)
- El Calafate → Torres del Paine (overland or tour, Chile)

---

## Notes

- Weather is highly variable, even in summer
- Wind can be strong throughout the region
- Hiking in El Chaltén is optional, not mandatory
