## Overview

A scenic trip to **Mendoza**, located at the foothills of the Andes and serving as the
main gateway to **Aconcagua**, the highest mountain in the Americas.

Mendoza combines **Andes landscapes, historic mountain routes, and relaxed city life**.
It is compact, easy to visit, and works well both as a standalone destination and as a connection point to Chile.

**Province:** Mendoza  
**Overall time:** 3–5 days  
**Main focus:** Andes scenery, viewpoints, mountain roads  
**Ranking:** strong regional highlight  
**Best season:** October–April (best for mountain routes; winter focuses more on city and scenery)

## Transportation

- **Best option:** rented car (maximum flexibility for mountain routes)
- Organized day tours are a good alternative if you prefer not to drive
- Public transport is limited outside the city
- Taxis and buses work well inside Mendoza

---

## Connections

- Mendoza → Santiago (bus via Andes or direct flight)
- Mendoza → Bariloche (direct flights, not daily)
- Mendoza → Salta (direct flights, not daily)

---

## Notes

- Altitude can affect some travelers on high Andes routes
- Weather changes quickly in the mountains
