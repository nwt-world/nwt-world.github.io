## Overview

A compact yet highly rewarding trip to **Ushuaia**, the southernmost city in the world,
surrounded by mountains, subantarctic forests, and the Beagle Channel.

Often known as the gateway to Antarctica, Ushuaia also works **very well as a standalone destination**
with excellent nature access and manageable logistics.

**Province:** Tierra del Fuego
**Base:** Ushuaia
**Overall time:** 5–6 days
**Main focus:** nature, easy hiking, maritime landscapes
**Ranking:** top Patagonian highlight
**Best season:** November–March

## Transportation

- Rental car is useful but **not required**
- Taxis are reliable for all excursions
- Shared transfers cover all major sights

---

## Connections

- Ushuaia → El Calafate (frequent direct flights)

---

## Notes

- Weather can change several times per day
- Wind is common year-round
- Rain and snow are possible even in summer
