## Overview

This itinerary covers Sri Lanka’s **main cultural and natural highlights**, combining:
- ancient sites in the Cultural Triangle,
- scenic hill country and one of the world’s most beautiful train rides,
- and relaxed beaches on the south coast.

The route is designed to be **logistically efficient**, with clear guidance on how to move between locations
using **train, bus, or private transfer**.

Total duration: **~10 days**, with the option to extend beach time.

## Best season

Sri Lanka’s weather varies by region.

General guidance:
- **December–March**: best for south coast and hill country
- **April–September**: better for the east coast
- Hill country is cooler year-round

This itinerary works best during the **winter**.
