## Overview

This itinerary covers **Southern Vietnam**, a region best visited during the winter.
It combines **tropical islands, coastal cities, cooler highlands, and urban culture**.

The route is flexible and modular:
- It works for a **short vacation**
- It scales well for **slow travel or long stays**
- Most distances are manageable by **domestic flights or buses**

## Best season

Southern Vietnam is best visited during:
- **December–March** – dry, warm, and relatively comfortable
- **April–May** – hotter, but still travelable
- **Rainy season (May–October)** – short tropical rains, fewer crowds

Phu Quoc and coastal areas are especially pleasant in winter.

---

## Who this itinerary is for

- Travelers seeking **beaches and warm weather in winter**
- Those who want a balance of **nature, relaxation, and culture**
- Flexible travelers planning **1–3+ weeks** in Vietnam
- Slow travelers and remote workers looking for variety

Southern Vietnam offers one of the most **comfortable and diverse introductions to the country**,
especially during the winter months.
