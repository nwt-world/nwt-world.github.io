## Overview

Why go:
- Talampaya is one of Argentina’s most distinctive canyon landscapes
- Ischigualasto adds moon-like desert scenery (Valle de la Luna)
- Laguna Brava gives a very different high-Andes day, with altitude, lagoons, and wide volcanic views

**Province:** La Rioja, San Juan  
**Overall time:** 6 days / 5 nights  
**Main focus:** canyons, desert parks, high-Andes scenery  
**Best season:** April–November

## Transportation

This itinerary works in three main styles:

### 1. Bus + excursions
Best if:
- you do not want to drive
- you are willing to solve the La Rioja → Villa Unión segment in advance

How it works:
- bus to Villa Unión
- excursions from Villa Unión for Talampaya, Ischigualasto, and Laguna Brava

### 2. Transfer + excursions
Best if:
- you want simpler logistics
- you are fine with paying more for comfort

How it works:
- private/shared transfer to Villa Unión
- excursion days from there

### 3. Rental car + selected excursions
Best if:
- you want more flexibility on transfer days
- you are comfortable with long desert drives

Important:
- even with a car, Talampaya still requires the official inside excursion
- Laguna Brava is not the kind of day you normally solve with an ordinary self-drive plan
