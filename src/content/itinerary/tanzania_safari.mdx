## Overview

A classic East African itinerary combining:

- Serengeti wildlife  
- Ngorongoro Crater  
- Additional northern circuit parks  
- Zanzibar island relaxation  

Important logistics note:

- This is **not a self-drive itinerary**.  
- Safaris are conducted with licensed driver-guides in 4x4 vehicles.  
- Distances between parks are large and handled within organized safari packages.

**Regions:** Northern Tanzania + Zanzibar  
**Overall time:** 10–14 days  
**Main focus:** wildlife + island  
**Best season:** June–October (dry season, best wildlife visibility)

Arrival airport:
- Kilimanjaro International Airport (JRO)

---

## Travel Logic (Important)

You choose between:

Option 1 – Overland Safari (Most Common)
- 4x4 vehicle with driver-guide
- Lodges or tented camps
- Road transfers between parks
- Ends back in Arusha

Option 2 – Fly-In Safari (Higher Budget)
- Small bush flights between parks
- Less driving time
- More expensive

After safari:
- Internal flight to Zanzibar (ZNZ)

No realistic independent transport between parks.

## Transportation Summary

Safari:
- Always via licensed safari operator
- 4x4 vehicle with driver-guide
- Park fees included in package

Between parks:
- Road transfers inside safari
- Optional bush flights

Safari → Zanzibar:
- Domestic flight strongly recommended

Zanzibar:
- No need for rental car
- Hotel-arranged transfers easiest
- Tours easily booked locally

---

## Cost Logic

Tanzania safari is expensive.

Main cost drivers:
- Park fees (especially Serengeti & Ngorongoro)
- Vehicle and guide
- Lodges

Fly-in safari significantly increases budget.

Zanzibar:
- Wide price range
- Beachfront accommodation varies from moderate to luxury

---

## Safety Notes

- Safari areas are controlled and safe
- Zanzibar is calm and tourist-friendly
- Stone Town requires normal city awareness
- Use hotel-arranged transfers

---

## Connections

- Easy combination with Kenya
- Flights to Dubai / Doha / Istanbul common
