## Overview

A short and extremely impactful visit to **Iguazú Falls**, one of the top natural wonders of South America.

**Province:** Misiones
**Overall time:** 1–2 nights
**Main focus:** waterfalls, rainforest
**Ranking:** top natural highlight
**Best season:** March–May, August–October (less rain, better visibility)

Iguazú is easy to visit and does not require a long stay.
One full day on the Argentine side already delivers the complete experience.

## Notes

- Rain increases water volume but can reduce visibility
