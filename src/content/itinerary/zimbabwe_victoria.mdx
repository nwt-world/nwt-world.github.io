## Overview

A classic African itinerary centered on Victoria Falls, one of the world’s most powerful waterfalls,
combined with optional wildlife experiences in Botswana.

Unlike Iguazú, this route is structured and tour-based by nature.
It works best when logistics are planned in advance and activities are bundled through reliable operators.

**Region:** Zimbabwe / Zambia / Botswana  
**Overall time:** 5–7 days  
**Main focus:** waterfalls, rivers, safari  
**Ranking:** iconic natural wonder + safari gateway  
**Best season:** May–October (dry season, best visibility)

## Transportation

- Flights connect Victoria Falls primarily with Johannesburg
- Transfers and tours handle long distances and borders
- Independent driving is not recommended
- Public transport is not suitable for this itinerary

---

## Safety Notes

- Stay within tourist areas
- Use organized transfers and tours
- Avoid night walking outside town
- Border crossings are safe when arranged professionally
